Network Readiness Index

The Networked Readiness Index measures, on a scale from 1 (worst) to 7 (best), the performance of 148 economies in leveraging information and communications technologies to boost competitiveness and well-being.

How to read this chart: Each circle on this graph represents a country. The size of the circle shows the population size. The color of each circle and their position on the X shows their NRI Index.

Source: World Economic Forum
